Sahara

The Sahara is a desert on the African continent. With an area of 9,200,000 square kilometres, it is the largest hot desert in the world and the third-largest desert overall, smaller only than the deserts of Antarctica and the northern Arctic. Largest hot desert comprising the rivers Nile and Niger and famous for its sand dune fields.
